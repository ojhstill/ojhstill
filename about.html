Owari is the alias of Oliver Still, an artist, producer, and photographer, from Tunbridge Wells, UK. With the project still in its preliminary stages, Owari has already found individuality. A detailed balance between organic textures and vibrant percussion displays his unique impression to the genre, exploring new ground with each production. An avid follower of the alternative and ambient genres, his influences cover a wide range of electronic pioneers, experimental sound artists, and modern-classical composers.

Gaining early support and praise from BBC Music Introducing, the project has gone on to receive ‘Record Of The Week’ and multiple plays on late night BBC Radio 1. Subsequently, this led to Owari being asked to officially remix MJ Cole’s single ‘Pictures In My Head’ at the end of 2017. Furthermore, ahead of its release, electronic artist Slow Magic selected Owari’s ‘A New Era’ from the debut EP to be apart of his ‘Imaginary Friends Mixtape’ for Apple Music.

Through this collective support arises the debut 'A New Era EP’; an initial and fundamental step in the project’s present state and ongoing development.
